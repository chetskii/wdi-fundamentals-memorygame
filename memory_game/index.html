Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Test and improve your memory with this fun, relaxing game. Make it more challenging by timing your results!
